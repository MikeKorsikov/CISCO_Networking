# Security Controls

Security controls - countermeasures to minimize security risks to organizational assets. 

Admin controls - procedures and policies
Technical controls - HW and SW related
Physical controls - separate people from systems

###

Functional security controls:
Preventive - stop unwanted and unauthorized activity
Deterrent - discourage something from happening
Detective - focus on the discovery of a security breach
Corrective - counteract something undesirable by restoring the system back
Recovery - more advanced capabilities over corrective 
Compensative -  options to other controls (substitution)
