# Incident responsible

Aim - limit the impact of the attack, assess damage, implement recovery procedure

NIST - national institute of standards and technology

Incident Policy elements:
- purpose and scope
- definitions
- structure, roles and responsibilities
- priorities
- performance measures
- reporting forms

Incident Response Plan elements:
- mission, strategies and goals
- approach to response
- communication plan
- metrics for response capacity

Incident Procedure elements:
- technical processes
- using techniques
- filling out forms
- checklists
