# Common Vulnerability Scoring System (CVSS)

CVSS - risk assessment tool to convey severity of vulnerabilities in HW and SW systems
FIRST -  Forum of Incident Response and Security Teams 

Groups of metrics:
- Base Metric Group (exploitability, impact)
- Temporal Metric Group
- Environmental Metric Group


CVSS processes: questionnaire > scoring

CVSS reports: 
Rating: none, low, medium, high, critical

CVE - Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures (by Mitre)
Dictionary of common names for known cybersecurity vulnerabilities.

NVD - National Vulnerability Database (by US NIST)
